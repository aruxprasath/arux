Dear Participants,

We are excited to invite you to participate in **Pixel Craft (UI/UX Design)**, organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and the Department of Information Technology, Arasu Engineering College. This competition aims to test your creativity and problem-solving skills in designing user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces using **Figma**.

Objective

The main objective of this competition is to test participants’ creativity and problem-solving skills in designing user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces using Figma.

What is UI/UX Design?

**UI (User Interface) Design** focuses on how an application looks and interacts with the user, while **UX (User Experience) Design** focuses on how the user feels when interacting with the application.

Benefits of UI/UX Design
  • Enhances user satisfaction and engagement.
  • Provides easy navigation and better accessibility.
  • Improves product branding and trust.
  • Saves development time and reduces cost.
Important

Problem statement will be given on the spot. Only Figma tool must be used for designing.

Rules
  • In-built templates are not allowed.
  • Browser can only be used for downloading images and icons.
  • Software to be used: Figma.
  • Maximum 3 pages only.
  • Time limit: 30 minutes.
  • Figma plugins are allowed.
  • If two or more pages are created, prototype flow is mandatory.
  • Punctuality is important. Late participants will not get extra time.
  • Participants can use their own laptops, but they must create a new prototype during the event.
