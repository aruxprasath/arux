ADZIA

DURATION:1.30 hrs

Date: Sep 24 2025

DESCRIPTION:

ADZIA is a fun and creative competition where participants form teams to present unique advertisements, skits, or roleplays on given topics in a humorous and engaging manner.

It encourages creativity, spontaneity, teamwork, and entertainment among the participants.

Rules:

  1. Each team can have 3 members
  2. Round 1: Event 1: Role Reversal - Teams act out by reversing roles (e.g., student as teacher, customer as shopkeeper).
  3. Round 2: Event 2: Crisis Management - Teams are given a problem situation and must perform how they handle it (e.g., they've to sell the product which is banned).

FROM ROUND 1 THE FIRST 5 TEAMS WILL BE LEAD TO THE FINAL ROUND

  1. FINAL Round 3: Surprise Round - Teams perform with a twist (the content will be provided for each team and on the spot twist will be given).
  2. Preparation time will be given before each round. (MAX 5 MNS )(All the contestants should complete with their timelimit or else it's invalid)
  3. Use of offensive or abusive content is strictly prohibited.
  4. Time limit must be followed strictly (For each team performance time will be 8 to 10 mns)
  5. Mobile phones are not allowed during the event. (no information should be gathered from internet or from friends or audience)
  6. Respect for fellow participants and judges is mandatory. (MARKS WILL BE BASED ON YOUR WAY OF APPROACH, ATTRACTIOM, HUMOUR)
