Master Minds – Rules and Regulations

Date: September 24, 2025

Dear Participants,

We are delighted to invite you to participate in Master Minds, a fun-filled intellectual challenge organized by the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and the Department of Information Technology, Arasu Engineering College. This event is designed to test participants' logical reasoning, quick thinking, and problem-solving ability through a series of engaging puzzle rounds.

■ Objective

Improve participants' logical and analytical skills. Sharpen quick-thinking ability through puzzles and riddles. Encourage teamwork, creativity, and fun learning in a competitive environment.

■ Event Format
Round Details Time Total Marks
Round 1 Crossword Puzzle (11 Q × 1 mark)
Connection (7 Q × 2 marks) 		15 min 25
Round 2 Rapid Fire (11 Q × 1 mark)
Jumbled Words (7 Q × 2 marks) 		15 min 25
■ Rules & Regulations

Participation can be individual or team of 2 members. All rounds are time-bound; no extra time will be given. Use of mobile phones, calculators, or external help is strictly prohibited. Only the answers submitted within time will be evaluated. There is no negative marking. In case of a tie, a sudden-death riddle/puzzle will decide the winner. Judges' and organizers' decisions will be final.
