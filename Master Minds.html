Rules & Regulations

  • Participation is strictly in teams of 2 members.
  • All rounds are time bound; no extra time will be given.
  • Use of mobile phones, calculators, or external help is strictly prohibited.
  • Only answers submitted within time will be evaluated.
  • No negative marking.
  • In case of a tie, a sudden-death riddle/puzzle will decide the winner.
  • Judges’ and organizers’ decisions will be final.
