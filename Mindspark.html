MindSpark – Technical Quiz

Event Objective

The Technical Quiz aims to test participants' knowledge and analytical skills in emerging technologies and fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Information Technology.

Date and Venue

September 24, 2025 – Arasu Engineering College, Auditorium.

Eligibility

Open to all students currently enrolled in undergraduate programs. On-spot registration is also available. Participants may register individually.

Registration Process

Participants must register before September 20, 2025. Registration can be done online or by contacting the organizing committee.

Quiz Structure

  • General Technical Round (Multiple-choice questions)
  • Subject-specific Round (AI, Data Science, IT concepts)

Time Limits

Each round will have a predefined time limit. Questions must be answered within the allocated time.

Decision Authority

The decision of the quiz master and organizing committee will be final and binding in case of disputes.

Code of Conduct

Participants must maintain decorum throughout the event. Use of electronic devices, notes, or external help is strictly prohibited.

Awards and Certificates

Certificates of participation will be provided to all participants.
